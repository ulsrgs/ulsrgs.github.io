ULSR-GS: Ultra Large-scale Surface Reconstruction Gaussian Splatting with Multi-View Geometric Consistency

-->

Abstract

We present ULSR-GS, a framework dedicated to high-fidelity surface extraction in ultra-large-scale scenes, addressing the limitations of existing Gaussian Splatting (GS) methods. While GS and related works demonstrate efficient and high-quality scene rendering and surface extraction, they fall short in handling large-scale surface extraction. To overcome this, we propose a point-to-photo partitioning approach combined with a multi-view optimal view matching principle to select the best training images for each sub-region. Additionally, during training, ULSR-GS employs a densification strategy based on multi-view geometric consistency to enhance rendering and surface extraction details. Experimental results demonstrate that ULSR-GS significantly improves both rendering quality and surface extraction accuracy in large-scale scenes.
